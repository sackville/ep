# Reducing the Cost of Reorientation

*Working draft placeholder.*

Enterprise software often fails not only because users get lost, but because they must repeatedly rebuild context after every interruption, meeting gap, or overnight pause.

A useful Enterprise Playability heuristic:

> The best enterprise applications reduce the cognitive cost of reorientation.

In Command, this becomes concrete: the board exists so the team can spend its time making decisions rather than reconstructing context.

Future notes:

- Reorientation is a recurring form of enterprise work.
- Shared understanding degrades over time unless deliberately preserved.
- Command acts as the team's collective working memory.
- The wake, decision records, rationale, objectives, and frontier all reduce the cost of returning to the work.
